## Description

Calculate the LCPD (least common prime divisor) of two numbers.

## Example

`leastCommonPrimeDivisor(12, 13) = -1`,
`leastCommonPrimeDivisor(12, 18) = 2`

-   [input] integer a
    positive integer
-   [input] integer b
    positive integer
-   [output] integer
    LCPD of a and b, -1 if it doesn't exist
